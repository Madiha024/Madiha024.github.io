What is Saraswati Puja / Vasant Panchami?

The goddess Saraswathi and the first day of spring are honored during this event. The Goddess of learning is Saraswathi. She is also the goddess of dance, drama, poetry, and music. In North India, this event is widely observed. The attire for this celebration is colorful. The warmth of spring is symbolized by yellow clothing.
