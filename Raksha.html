What is Raksha Bandhan?

At Raksha Bandhan, brothers and sisters show their love for each other. Every sister marks her brother's forehead with a special paste. Then she puts rice grains on the mark. She ties a rakhi (bracelet made from thread) around her brother's wrist. Rakhi's symbolically protect them. After that sister's receive a gift in return.
