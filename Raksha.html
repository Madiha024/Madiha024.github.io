What is Raksha Bandhan?

Brothers and sisters express their love for one another during Raksha Bandhan. Every sister uses a unique paste to mark her brother's forehead. Then she marks the area with rice grains. She fastens a thread bracelet known as a rakhi around her brother's wrist. They are metaphorically shielded by rakhis. Sisters receive a gift in response after that.
