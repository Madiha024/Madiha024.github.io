What is Krishna Janmashtami?

It's a joyful occasion honoring Krishna's birth.Krishna, according to Hindu tradition, was born at midnight. People get together at the mandir(temple) in the evening . They rotate the lamps in front of the murtis (statues). The aarti ceremony is being held right now. People dance and chant religious tunes. Many Hindus fast from sunrise to sunset. At midnight, they exchange fruits and sweets or a substantial supper.
