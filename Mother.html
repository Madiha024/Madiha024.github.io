What is Mother's Day and why is it celebrated?

Mother's Day is a special day celebrated around the world to honor and appreciate the love, care, and sacrifices of mothers. The day is observed on different dates in various countries, but it is usually celebrated on the second Sunday of May in most parts of the world.

On Mother's Day, people typically honor their mothers by giving them cards, flowers, gifts, or other tokens of appreciation. It's also common for families to spend time together, whether it's through a special meal or outing or simply spending time at home.
